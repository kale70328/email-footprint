# Privacy Snippet

This is the exact copy used in the Email Footprint UI.

---

## Short privacy notice (shown on Home page and before verification)

> We do not store your email or results beyond this session. All data is held in your browser only
> and automatically cleared after **5 minutes** of inactivity or when you click "Clear session".
> Results are inferences from public breach data — not confirmations. This app never requests your
> password.

---

## Tooltip / modal copy (shown when user clicks "How are results calculated?")

Email Footprint queries publicly available breach datasets and other open data sources to infer
which services may be associated with your email address.

Sources include breach feeds such as **Have I Been Pwned** and similar public datasets. Data is
cross-referenced by email domain and username patterns.

**Important:** All results are probabilistic inferences. A "Possible" result means your email
appeared in breach data associated with a service — it does not confirm you have an active
account there.

This service never requests your password, and results are only shown within your current session.

---

## Verification screen notice

> The verification code is sent only to confirm this email belongs to you. We do not store your
> email or results beyond this session.

---

## Results disclaimer (shown above account cards)

> **Disclaimer:** These results are inferences based on publicly known breach data. A "Possible"
> label means your email appeared in breach data associated with that service — it is not a
> confirmation of an active account. This app never stores your data beyond this session.
